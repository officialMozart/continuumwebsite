Who are we?

We are a team of developers. We have made products such as: anti autoclicker, diskord, timber & veinminer, diamond essentials and more!

Anti Autoclicker

A small and effective script to warn you about autoclickers! It has functions to freeze players or kick them if you so wish.

Diskord

This is a bare-bones script which has everything you need for a simple discord bot, it is not bloated like other notable minecraft-to-discord plugin, discordsrv, but has only the essentials for those we need those.

Timber & Veinminer

The perfect plugin you'll NEED for your SMP server. This plugin auto-chops the trees and auto-mines the veins so that you dont need to waste your time. Perfect for a SMP!
